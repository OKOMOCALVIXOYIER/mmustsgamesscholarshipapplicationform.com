MMUST Games Department Scholarship Terms and Policy

Eligibility for Scholarship:

  • Must be a registered student of Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology.
  • Actively participating and representing the University in a recognized game/sport under the Games Department.
  • Demonstrate a strong commitment to both academic studies and athletic training/competition.
  • Must adhere to the rules and regulations set forth by the MMUST Games Department and relevant sporting bodies.

Scholarship Obligations:

  • Recipients are expected to maintain a satisfactory academic performance as determined by the University.
  • Must actively participate and represent MMUST in scheduled games and competitions.
  • Must attend mandatory training sessions and team meetings as required by the coach.
  • Must uphold the good image and reputation of the University and the Games Department.
  • Any withdrawal from the University team or failure to meet the obligations may result in the revocation of the scholarship.
  • Recipients may be required to participate in promotional activities for the University Games Department.

Application Process:

  • All sections of the application form must be completed accurately and truthfully.
  • Submission of the required documents (National ID/Passport, Academic Transcript, KCSE/Equivalent Result Slip, Letter of Support) is mandatory.
  • The Games Department Scholarship Committee will review all applications.
  • Shortlisted candidates may be invited for an interview.
  • The decision of the Scholarship Committee is final and binding.

Data Privacy:

Personal information provided in this application will be used solely for the purpose of scholarship selection and management by the MMUST Games Department and relevant University offices. Your data will be handled with confidentiality in accordance with the University's data protection policies.
